# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Technical recruiters, engineering leads, founders, and AI practitioners reviewing Parth Thakre's work. They need to quickly understand whether the work is real, inspectable, and relevant to enterprise AI systems.

## Product Purpose

Present Parth as an AI Systems Engineer who builds auditable LLM systems, retrieval agents, model-evaluation rigs, and private runtime infrastructure. Success means visitors can identify the strongest projects, verify credibility, and reach out or download the resume without friction.

## Brand Personality

Alpine, exacting, operational. The interface should feel like an expedition route map for AI systems work with a Celeste-adjacent mountain mood: technical, calm under pressure, evidence-driven, and quietly personal.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic AI SaaS dashboards, decorative gradient blobs, flat card grids, vague AI buzzword copy, and portfolio pages that hide the actual work behind marketing language. Do not make the trans palette look accidental or tokenistic; use blue, pink, and white as the core visual weather of the page.

## Design Principles

- Show the route: every major claim should connect to a project, trace, citation, scorecard, or runtime decision.
- Lead with terrain: use a decisive mountain visual instead of generic abstract AI art, closer to a difficult climb than a corporate hero.
- Keep the proof close: project impact, stack, and verification details should be visible without hunting.
- Preserve parser access: HTML, Markdown, and PDF resume paths remain first-class.
- Respect both viewing modes: dark mode should feel native, and light mode should not be an afterthought.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast, keyboard-accessible navigation, reduced-motion fallbacks, semantic sections, and readable responsive type across mobile and desktop.
