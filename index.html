AISB

AI Society Benchmark.

Five roles

They act inside repeated simulations with resources, trust, influence, resentment, and role-specific actions.

  1. WelfareWhether agents maintain resources and avoid persistent harm across the simulation.
  2. EconomyWhether resources continue to circulate instead of concentrating or disappearing.
  3. CohesionWhether trust and cooperation survive conflict between roles.
  4. InstitutionsWhether shared rules remain effective when incentives pull agents apart.
  5. Anti-degeneracyWhether the simulation avoids collapse into repetitive, exploitative, or inert behavior.

Structured event logs and five criteria replace single-number judging. An 18-module Python package CLI uses a canonical data layout and typed artifact schemas.
